# Financial-Modelling-Valuation-Infosys-Excel

Financial Modelling & DCF Valuation of Infosys Ltd. using Microsoft Excel, featuring 10 years of historical financial analysis, ratio analysis, financial forecasting, and intrinsic valuation.

## Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive Financial Modelling and Valuation of Infosys Ltd. built in Microsoft Excel. The model analyses 10 years of historical financial statements to evaluate the company's growth, profitability, and operational efficiency. It includes common-size financial statements, ratio analysis, financial forecasting, and intrinsic valuation using the Discounted Cash Flow (DCF) method. The model follows a structured approach commonly used in equity research and corporate finance.

## Key Insights

- Analysed 10 years of historical financial data, where revenue increased from ₹62,441 Cr to ₹1,62,990 Cr, resulting in a CAGR of approximately 10%.
- Evaluated profitability trends through EBITDA and Net Profit Margins, observing gradual margin compression over the period.
- Identified improvement in Return on Capital Employed (ROCE) from approximately 25% to 33%, indicating enhanced capital efficiency.
- Performed common-size analysis to assess cost structure, profitability, and balance sheet composition.
- Forecasted Revenue, EBITDA, and Earnings Per Share (EPS) using historical growth trends and weighted average growth assumptions.
- Estimated the intrinsic value of the company using the Discounted Cash Flow (DCF) valuation method.
